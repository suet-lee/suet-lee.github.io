about

I am an interdisciplinary researcher working in swarm/multi-robot systems and complexity across scales. The design of swarm systems is often inspired by patterns and behaviours found in biological collectives, from insects and animals to human society. The symmetry and patterns which emerge from swarm/complex systems can be very beautiful. I try to understand these systems, am inspired by them, and in turn, design them towards constructive purposes.
